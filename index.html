Warrior

A medieval warrior was a professional soldier, often a noble or knight, who fought on horseback or on foot, typically wearing armor and armed with a sword, spear, or axe. Their main role was to defend lands, participate in battles, and serve a feudal lord or king.

Dwarf Warrior

A short, stout, and immensely resilient fighter from the underground halls of Middle-earth, characterized by a full beard and heavy, expertly crafted armor. They are considered the most formidable warriors among the Free Peoples, known for their fierce, unyielding combat style and preference for axes and hammers over swords.

Wizard

A divine, immortal spirit (Maia) sent by the Valar (High Powers) to Middle-earth in human form to aid and advise the Free Peoples against the rising power of Sauron.
